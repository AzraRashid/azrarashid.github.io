FitPulse Wellness.

Overview

Active living, by design.

The brand mark.

The logo represents the brand identity. FitPulse features a modern monogram with a pulse element — representing active living, tracking, and transformation through fitness.

The product is driven by movement and mindful habits, focused on small daily actions that keep users active, energized, and productive.

Design Process

Research, ideate, design.

A three-phase process anchored around real B2B user needs — gyms and companies looking to improve employee health through scalable, measurable wellness tools.

FP
01

Research

Target users analysis

B2B users for a fitness app are gyms and companies that want to improve employee health. They look for simple, scalable tools that boost engagement and deliver measurable wellness results.

02

Ideate

Brainstorm & align

In this phase I ran brainstorming sessions, discussed solution paths, and used user research to align ideas with real needs — refining the strongest direction before any pixels were moved.

03

Design

Wireframes → UI

Created wireframes to plan structure and flow, then developed UI concepts to explore layout and functionality. Visual design was applied last — shaping the final look and feel into a clean, user-friendly experience.

Problem & Solution

Two problems, one platform.

Problem 01

Workplaces face declining productivity due to stress, burnout, and excessive screen time. Using devices for long hours reduces focus, while busy schedules make it hard to maintain healthy habits.

Solution

A fitness and wellness engagement platform that improves user productivity by embedding short, actionable health habits into the workday — through gamification and rewards.

Problem 02

Traditional wellness programs often see low engagement and weak results. This leads to rising burnout, absenteeism, and higher costs — creating a gap between users' well-being efforts and real productivity outcomes.

Outcome

Higher daily engagement, lower drop-off, and a clear feedback loop between healthy actions and tangible workplace benefits.

Make wellness easy, quick, and rewarding during work hours.
